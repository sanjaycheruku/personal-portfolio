    About ResumeAI
    
      
    ResumeAI is an intelligent ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between talented candidates and their dream roles. By leveraging advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning, we provide data-driven insights that help you optimize your professional narrative for modern Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).
    
      
      
    
        Smart Scoring
        NLP Parsing

    How It Works
    
      
    
        
    
          
    📂
    
          
    Document Analysis
    
          
    Our backend extracts semantic content from PDF, DOCX, and TXT files using specialized parsers like pdfminer and python-docx.
    
        
    
        
    
          
    🧠
    
          
    AI Engine
    
          
    We use spaCy and scikit-learn for Named Entity Recognition (NER) and TF-IDF analysis to identify skills and calculate relevance scores.
    
        
    
        
    
          
    ⚖️
    
          
    ATS Simulation
    
          
    We simulate the scoring algorithms used by top-tier ATS platforms to give you an accurate "Recruiter's View" of your resume.
    
        
    
        
    
          
    📄
    
          
    Live Rendering
    
          
    Our editor allows for real-time visualization of your changes, with final export capabilities in both PDF and DOCX formats.
